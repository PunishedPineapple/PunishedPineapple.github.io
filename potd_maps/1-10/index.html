Floors 1-10

Instructions

POTD floors are generated as subsets of a master floor layout. The master floor layouts are 21 rooms layed out in a 5x5 grid (with the four corners of the grid not containing a room). There are two master floor layouts that can be chosen between (Called 'A' and 'B' here).

Once a master floor layout is chosen, any number of contiguous rooms are chosen, and all other rooms from the master layout are sealed off.

In order to help identify rooms, thumbnails of each room from above are laid out in the master grid. Cell background colors are the color that the room's ambient light is tinted (where applicable). Hallway shapes between rooms are also shown. At one point, SE had stated that they changed hallways into rooms with beacons to be straight. I am not certain if this means that some hallways shapes may be invalid, or if it affected where beacons are placed (or neither).

The following colors are used to indicate locations on the maps:

  • Green: Hoard Locations (with visual evidence provided)
  • Red: Trap Locations (hoards may or may not be able to spawn here)
  • Blue: Unverified Hoard Locations (i.e. screenshots not showing a marker or coffer)

Clicking on a room's thumbnail will open the full-sized image for that room.
