Treasure Information

Personal chests in the field appear to have fixed spawn locations. There are likely eight silver coffers and 60 bronze coffers. This map shows the locations of those coffers, and any carrot or imp coffer spawns observed so far. The number of possible carrot and imp coffer locations is unknown, and many are probably missing from this map.
