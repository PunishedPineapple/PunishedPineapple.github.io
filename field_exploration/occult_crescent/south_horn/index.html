South Horn

Treasure Locations

Personal chests in the field appear to have fixed spawn locations. There are likely eight silver coffers and 60 bronze coffers. This map shows the locations of those coffers, and any carrots or imp coffer locations observed so far. The number of possible carrot spawns and imp coffer locations is unknown, and many are probably missing from this map.
