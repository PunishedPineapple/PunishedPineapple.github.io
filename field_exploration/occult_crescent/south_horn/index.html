South Horn

Treasure Locations

Personal chests in the field appear to have fixed spawn locations. There are likely eight silver coffers and 60 bronze coffers. This map shows the locations of those coffers, and any carrots observed so far. The number of possible carrot spawns is unknown, and many are probably missing from this map.

Known spawn locations for imp coffers are also shown in a separate image below. Like for carrot spawns, this map is incomplete.

  • Blue Circle: Silver Coffer Spawns
  • Red Circle: Bronze Coffers Spawns
  • Orange Triangle: Fortune Carrot Spawns
